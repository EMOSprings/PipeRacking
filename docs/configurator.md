# 3D Configurator

This page will host the interactive 3D tool for designing and ordering modular racking.

*(Development in progress)*

---

### Core Features:

*   **Real-time Visualization:** See your design come to life as you add, remove, and adjust components.
*   **Instant Pricing:** Get immediate cost feedback as you configure your solution.
*   **One-Click Ordering:** Seamlessly transition from design to purchase.
